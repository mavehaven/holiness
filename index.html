Advanced 3D Technologies

Discover the spectrum of stereoscopic experiences we offer, from accessible anaglyph to cutting-edge glasses-free solutions

Anaglyph 3D

The most accessible form of stereoscopic 3D, using color filters to separate images for each eye. Perfect for mass distribution and viewers with basic red-blue or green-magenta glasses.

Basic color-filter glasses required
Works on all devices and screens
Mass audience accessibility
Ideal for indie films and home use

Polarized 3D

Professional-grade stereoscopic experience using polarized light to separate images for each eye. Delivers superior image quality and depth perception for premium viewing experiences.

Requires 3D projectors or displays
Cinema-quality viewing experience
Enhanced depth and visual impact
Industry standard for professional 3D

Autostereoscopic 3D

The peak of 3D viewing, absolutely no glasses required! Using parallax barrier or lenticular lens technology to deliver a seamless, immersive experience directly to your eyes.

Completely glasses-free experience
Requires specialized displays
Premium cutting-edge technology
Ultimate immersive experience
